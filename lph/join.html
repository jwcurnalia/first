Member Benefits

  • Receipt of silver and crimson honors cords for graduation
  • Participation in an organization devoted to your professional development
  • A link to the nation-wide network of Lambda Pi Eta members
  • Ability to put Lambda Pi Eta membership on resumes


Requirements for Membership

  • Applicants should be Communication or Telecommunication Majors
  • Minimum of 12 semester hours in COMST or TCOMM
  • Minimum GPA of 3.25 in your Major
  • Minimum Overall GPA of 3.00
  • $35 Membership Fee
